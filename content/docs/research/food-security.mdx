## Definition

According to **food security** defined by FAO [#(3)](#3), national food security implies that **a**
**country has sufficient food for everyone**.

Being the Agriculture sector, the responsible for providing "*food for everyone*", it is of great importance to have in mind all the possible threats and conditions that could destabilise the system for this to happen.

As stated on this research from Li C [#(1)](#1), *"ensuring food security requires not only addressing*
*the most fundamental and direct factors but also incorporating a broader range of indirect factors that may impact food security into the research."* That means not only taking into account climate change, economic security or social changes, but also [Cybersecurity in CEA](./cybersecurity-in-cea.mdx) and Resource efficient systems for the [Controlled Environment Agriculture](./controlled-environment-agriculture.mdx) to be much more sustainable in the long run.
