## What is an Attack Surface?

An organization’s attack surface is the sum of vulnerabilities, pathways, or methods—sometimes called attack vectors—that hackers can use to gain unauthorized access to the network or sensitive data, or to carry out a cyberattack [#(1)](#1).

It is the main factor to take in mind when talking about [Cybersecurity in CEA](./cybersecurity-in-cea.mdx).

Since [Precision Agriculture](./precision-agriculture.mdx) is mostly filled with digital assets, the new ecosystem provides a huge set of opportunities for Hackers to deliver a wide range of possible attacks.

***

## Possible sub-surfaces

1. Digital attacks
2. Physical attacks
3. Social Engineering
