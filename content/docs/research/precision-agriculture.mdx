## The Language of Nature

Precision Agriculture is about understanding
the language of nature.

Each sensor is listening to the information that each plant
or microorganism is saying.

<ObsidianCallout type="info">
  <ObsidianCalloutTitle>
    Definition
  </ObsidianCalloutTitle>

  <ObsidianCalloutBody>
    Precision agriculture comprises a set of technologies that combines sensors, information systems, enhanced machinery, and informed management to optimize production by accounting for variability and uncertainties within agricultural systems.[Precision Agriculture and Food Security](https://www.science.org/doi/abs/10.1126/science.1183899)
  </ObsidianCalloutBody>
</ObsidianCallout>

Inside a [Controlled Environment Agriculture](./controlled-environment-agriculture.mdx), Precision Agriculture helps to provide the necessary information to create the same optimal conditions for the plant to grow.

> PA is conceptualized by a system approach to re-organize the total system of agriculture towards a low-input, high-efficiency, sustainable agriculture (Shibusawa, 1998)
